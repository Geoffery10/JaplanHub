This is a list of requirements before you go to Japan. You must have all of these completed if relevant or not optional before you go.

Finance 💸

  • Ticket Funded Before (May 1st) 🎫
  • Hotel Funded (August 1st) 🏨
  • Finish Funding (At least 1 Week Before Flight)
  • Notify your bank of travel to and around Japan, including layovers, and ensure the ability to use international ATMs.
    • Check if your credit/debit cards have any foreign transaction fees. (Note you will be provided with Yen for each day that should cover everything except excess purchases)

Tasks 📃

  • Get Passport (February 1st) 🛂
    • Take photocopies of your passport and important documents and keep them separately.
    • Send a copy to Geoffery so he can book international flights.
  • Check with your cell phone provider for international options. (We can also get SIM cards in Japan)
  • Complete Packing Guide 🧳

Cultural Awareness 🎎

  • (Recommended): Learn a few basic Japanese phrases; it's appreciated by the locals.
  • (Recommended): Familiarize yourself with Japanese customs and etiquette, especially in places like temples and traditional accommodations.

Entertainment 🎭

  • (Recommended): Look up points of interest and add them to the poi chats on Discord. Ideas include restaurants, interesting places, events/festivals, or exhibitions.
