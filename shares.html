The Why

Why Am I Sending Money to Geoffery?

1. Centralized Purchases: Geoffery will be managing all major group expenses, including train passes, accommodations, flights, and any additional passes or costs that may arise. Pooling our funds allows us to secure group housing and make coordinated bookings, making the logistics of our trip more seamless.

2. Currency Distribution: To enhance convenience during our travels, we plan to distribute funds in Japanese Yen. While you can still use your personal funds if you prefer, having a common pool ensures that everyone has enough resources for the entire trip without unexpected financial hiccups.
