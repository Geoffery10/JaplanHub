How to Apply for a U.S. Passport

If you haven’t applied yet, here’s what you’ll need:

1️⃣ Required Documents:

  • ✔ Proof of U.S. Citizenship: Certified birth certificate or certificate of naturalization
  • ✔ Proof of Identity: State-issued driver’s license or ID card, military ID, or government employee ID

2️⃣ Passport Photo:

You can get this at Walgreens, CVS, UPS stores, and many post offices.

3️⃣ Application Form:

Form DS-11 (for first-time applicants) — do not sign it until instructed at your appointment! You can fill it out online and print it, or print a blank copy and fill by hand.

4️⃣ Payment:

USPS charges a $35 processing fee and a $15 photo fee (if you choose to take your photo there.) A US passport will be $130, a passport card $30 and both $160. You can have this service expedited for an additional $60, and pay an additional $22.05 for 1-2 day delivery AFTER the passport has been mailed out.

5️⃣ Where to Apply:

Most post offices, county clerk offices, and passport acceptance facilities. Many locations require an appointment — book ASAP. We recommend USPS.

6️⃣ Processing Times:

Routine: 6–8 weeks
